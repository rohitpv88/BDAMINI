Crop Diseases Prediction:

The crop diseases prediction system is an AI-driven platform that uses deep learning, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to identify and predict plant diseases from leaf images. By analyzing visual patterns, the system accurately detects diseases early, enabling timely interventions to prevent crop damage. Implemented using Python Flask, it offers a user-friendly interface for farmers and agricultural professionals to upload images and receive disease diagnoses and management recommendations.
